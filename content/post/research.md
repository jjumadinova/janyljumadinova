# Research

## Ethical CS

Over the last three years I along with my colleagues in the [Department of Computer Science at Allegheny College](https://www.cs.allegheny.edu/), have been working on the Responsible Computer Science effort. {{< youtube uvXBEaByh6k >}} {{< youtube rmX7gCEkpQw >}}

## Monitoring with Robots

### System Monitoring

We are currently building a hardware/software solution that is capable of monitoring a complete system. Our solution consists of a multi-sensor unit equipped with predictive modeling software. We are testing our solution on an aquaponics system.

### Water Monitoring

Underwater robotic unit equipped with multiple sensors that are able to collect data autonomously. [Read an Allegheny news story.](https://sites.allegheny.edu/news/2019/02/19/allegheny-faculty-student-team-helps-develop-underwater-robots-to-perform-lake-research/) {{< youtube XLj_H2UTrLg >}}

### Well-being Monitoring

A system comprising of multiple robots and wearable sensors with a goal of monitoring a person's well-being.. [Read an Allegheny news story.](https://sites.allegheny.edu/news/2015/08/28/looking-for-a-health-coach-try-a-robot/) {{< youtube TDzP5kkIrro >}}
